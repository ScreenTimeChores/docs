Manage your kid's screen time by encouraging them to complete their tasks before diving into the virtual world.

Empowering parents and kids to manage screen time in a fun and productive way! This app helps parents encourage responsibility by linking screen time access to task completion. Parents can create customized tasks, setting app restrictions to ensure kids focus on their responsibilities first. Kids, in turn, can track their progress, earn rewards, and enjoy screen time as a well-deserved treat once their tasks are done. It's an engaging tool for building good habits, fostering accountability, and adding excitement to daily routines!
